Supreme Shippers seized its first opportunity to accommodate an Out of Gauge Cargo into In Gauge thus marking its entry in ODC project division.

Supreme Shippers plays a key role in transport of ODC cargos by road all over Dubai. This was possible by leasing ODC trucks as required and by deploying its own dedicated team to conduct road survey to give expertise suggestions for smooth movement of the ODC cargos.

Supreme Shippers specializes in providing customized solutions for the safe conveyance of over dimensional, heavy and bulky cargo. We have been responsible for the movement of cargo requiring unusual handling.

Supreme Shippers can arrange for Over-Dimensional Cargo to move overseas while lashed and choked on flat racks or open-top containers. This is ideal for large equipment and machinery.

For over-dimensional loads travelling over the road,GCE offers specialized heavy lift forklifts and the necessary equipment to secure them.
